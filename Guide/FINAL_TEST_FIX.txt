================================================================================
WHY THIS FIX WORKS
================================================================================

DatasetService.getIngestionStats() method calls:
-------------------------------------------------
1. customerRepository.count()           ✅ Was autowired
2. orderRepository.count()              ✅ Was autowired  
3. orderItemRepository.count()          ❌ WAS MISSING → NOW FIXED
4. paymentRepository.count()            ✅ Was autowired
5. productRepository.count()            ❌ WAS MISSING → NOW FIXED
6. sellerRepository.count()             ✅ Was autowired
7. deliveryRepository.count()           ❌ WAS MISSING → NOW FIXED
8. faultyRecordRepository.count()       ❌ WAS MISSING → NOW FIXED

Without all repositories autowired, when the test calls getIngestionStats(),
Spring tries to inject DatasetService, which needs all 8 repositories.
Since 4 were missing, they were null, causing NullPointerException.

Now all 8 are autowired, so DatasetService gets all its dependencies! ✅
